# AirBear Product Context

## Why AirBear Exists

### The Problem We're Solving
- **Transportation Emissions**: Traditional ride-sharing contributes significantly to carbon emissions
- **Urban Mobility**: Binghamton lacks efficient, eco-friendly transportation options
- **Local Business Support**: Small businesses struggle to reach customers outside traditional storefronts
- **Sustainable Infrastructure**: Limited infrastructure for zero-emission transportation solutions

### Market Gap
Current ride-sharing platforms don't address:
- Environmental sustainability as a core feature
- Local business integration with transportation
- PWA-first mobile experiences
- Regional optimization for smaller markets like Binghamton

## How AirBear Works

### Core User Journey
1. **Discovery**: User downloads/installs PWA, creates account
2. **Booking**: Selects destination on interactive map, chooses ride preferences
3. **Matching**: AI matches with available solar-powered AirBear and driver
4. **Ride Experience**: Zero-emission transportation with onboard shopping option
5. **Payment & Review**: Seamless checkout for ride and optional purchases
6. **Eco-Impact**: Clear visibility into personal environmental contributions

- **PWA Optimization**: Sub-second load times, offline functionality
